We are a friendly church community in Hounslow, West London.

We are community of people who have found that life has real meaning through faith in Jesus.

Covid-19 has affected the way we do church just as it has affected many other areas of life, but it has not stopped us from worshipping, praying, supporting one another and learning together. We are currently using a combination of Online and 'In-person' gatherings. Do check out our services on Youtube or check back here to see the next service details.
